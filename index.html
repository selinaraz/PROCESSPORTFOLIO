ABOUT ME

I'm Selin, a 21-year-old product designer based in Odense with a flair for crafting intuitive interfaces.

My passion for creative expression is a significant part of my life, a passion I actively pursue through my studies as “multimediedesigner” at UCL. Throughout my education, I have acquired skills in various Adobe programs, including Photoshop, Illustrator, InDesign, Xd, Premiere Pro, and Lightroom. My proficiency extends to HTML and CSS, alongside Wordpress, equipping me with the expertise to develop websites that are not only visually appealing but also user-friendly.

While I have a broad skill set, my true zeal lies in the realms of web design and app development, where I blend aesthetics with functionality to create seamless digital experiences.

If you're looking to elevate your content or bring fresh, innovative ideas to life, I invite you to reach out to me. My expertise in design and digital media is not just a service, but a partnership to transform your vision into reality. Whether it's revamping your branding, enhancing your online presence, or creating engaging multimedia content, I'm here to provide tailored solutions that resonate with your audience.

For a glimpse into my creative world and the work I've accomplished, please scroll further down the page to view my portfolio. Your journey towards impactful and memorable design starts with a simple conversation – feel free to contact me further down to explore the endless possibilities.
